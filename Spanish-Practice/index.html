How To Play

Begin by selecting how many questions you want to study in a round, then select a level to practice.

The word will appear in the chat bubble at the top of the game window. You must correctly translate the word into English by typing the word into the answer bar at the bottom.

Your word must match the spelling of the english word stored in the database. If a word is spelled wrong, check the logs and report an issue if you think there was a mistake.

If you chose the option "English to Spanish Translations", you must answer in Spanish instead, based on the English prompt.

Once you are finished, a list of any mistakes you may have made during the round will appear under the game client.

Please report all issues to the Github Repository
